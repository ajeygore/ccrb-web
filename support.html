Support for CruiseControl.rb

CruiseControl.rb is not a commercial piece of software, so we cannot guarantee support. However, we do have some ways to help you with issues you may have: If you subscribe to the ccrb-user mailing list , and post any query you may have somebody will usually come back with a response fairly quickly. You can also use the ccrb-user mailing list to provide feedback (improvement ideas, feature requests, etc.) We will be putting a FAQ and a Documentation page pretty soon on this website.

Please do not send email to committers asking for support

About Continuous Integration

Martin Fowler, ThoughtWorks' chief scientist, has a very nice article about it on his bliki

From Wikipedia
Continuous integration (CI) implements continuous processes of applying quality control — small pieces of effort, applied frequently. Continuous integration aims to improve the quality of software, and to reduce the time taken to deliver it, by replacing the traditional practice of applying quality control after completing all development.
